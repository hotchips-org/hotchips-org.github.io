# Parking


Please print out[ this pass](http://www.hotchips.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/ParkingPassV1.pdf) and bring it with you to Hot Chips. When you arrive, be prepared to show it to the parking attendants and please leave it on your dashboard while you are parked. If lost, additional passes will be available from Hot Chips staff in the Memorial Auditorium Lobby.

This pass will allow free parking **ONLY in the Galvez Field parking lot (see map) on August 25-27**. It is NOT valid for parking anyplace else on Stanford campus. To park elsewhere, you will need to use the standard parking meters or machines, instead.


# Breaks and Meals


Breakfast (pasteries, fruit, coffee and other refreshments) and tutorial & session breaks will be held on the east side of Memorial Auditorium (see map below (coffee cup icon)).

Lunches and the wine reception will be held in Dohrmann Grove, a short walk southwest from Memorial Auditorium (see map below (fork & spoon icon)).


# Wi-Fi and Power Outlets


Wi-Fi access will be available and the network name and password will be displayed on the auditorium screen during intermissions. Power outlets will also be available while you are seated in the auditorium.
